Brain teasers and puzzles
Jolanta Zurek 

        Do you love solving puzzles and thinking outside the box? You probably have heard of Wordle, but how about a Murdle? In this elective, you'll tackle a variety of brain-teasing challenges, from logic grids and number puzzles to deduction, word and hands-on puzzles. Each session will introduce a new type of puzzle, helping you develop problem-solving skills, critical thinking, and creativity in a fun and interactive way!
